Mission Statement

We created this website to bring awareness to mental health for women. Statistics show more than one in five women experience mental health conditions such as depression or anxiety within the US. However many of these conditions go undiagnosed in women since these symptoms may present themselves in different ways than men. By creating this website we hope to promote further awareness and insight into how mental illnesses affect different aspects of daily life.
