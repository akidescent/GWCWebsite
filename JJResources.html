Resources

  • To Write Love On Her Arms

    • TWLOHA is a non-profit movement with the mission to present hope and find help for people who are struggling with depression, self-injury, addiction, and suicide.

  • National Alliance for Mental Illness

    • NAMI is the largest mental health orginization in the United States. Through programs that promote education, advocacy, and support for people who experience mental illness, NAMI works to help the one in five people living with an mental illness in the United States.

  • Project Semicolon

    • The message of Project Semicolon is simple,"A semicolon represents a sentence the author could have ended, but chose not to. The author is you and the sentence is your life."

  • This Is My Brave

    • This Is My Brave is working to end mental health stigma though storytelling. The orginization empowers people to share their stories with mental illness through creative work.

  • Brain & Behavior Research Founder

    • The BBRFoundation is working to support mental health by providing grants for scientific research, helping discover better treatment and encourage better education about mental illness.

  • Treatment Advocacy Center

    • Treatment Advocacy Center is helping eliminate barriers when it comes to people reciving the mental health treatment they need.

  • Mental Health America

    • One of the country's leading community-based orginizations dedicated to supporting people with mental illness and promoting mental health nationally.
