Depression

Definition

A mental health disorder is characterized by a persistently depressed mood or loss of interest in activities, causing significant impairment in one's daily life.

Effects on Women

Depression is twice as likely to affect women's factors include reproductive hormones, a differing female response to stress, and social pressures that are uniquue to a woman's life experiences.

Common Forms of Depression in Women

  • Major Depression
    • Lose happiness in activities one would find enjoyable before.
  • Postpartum Depression or "Baby Blues"
    • This occurs after a woman gives birth or in the months leading up to giving birth.
  • Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder
    • Ties in with a woman's menstrual cycle.

How it differentiates between men and women

  • Women feel anxious and scared; men feel guarded
  • Women blame themselves for their depression; men blame others
  • Women commonly feel sad, worthless, and apathetic when depressed; men tend to feel irritable and angry
  • Women are more liekly avoid conflicts when depressed; men are more likelyto create conflict
  • Women feel lethsrgic and nervous; men feel agitated and restless
  • Women easily talk about their feelings of self-doubt and despair, considering it a sign of weakness

Symptoms

  • Feelings of emptiness and sadness
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Suicidal thoughts or attempts of suicide
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Cramps or Tender Breasts
  • Lack of Energy
  • Feeling out of control
  • Mood Swings
  • Panic attacks
  • Disinterest in daily activities and relationships

How to Seek Medical Help

  • Consult your doctor if you fear you may have depression
  • Don't keep your feelings bottled up - find a support group with a people you trust

Self-Help Options

While seeking for professional help is recommended; finding self-help options is also very recommded for people coping depression

The following are helpful coping mechainsms

  • Healthy eating habits
  • Exercising
  • Have a Healthy Sleeping Schedule
  • Have A Good Support System
  • Do Things That Make You Feel Good
