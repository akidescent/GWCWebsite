About Us

Purpose:

To bring awareness of mental health to the lives of women.

Being young women, we understand how much pressure we are put through in society, and the significance of recognizing and understanding personal mental health in a informative and positive way.

We also understand how hard it can be finding information and credible hotlines for mental health awareness, and wished to provide a constructive platform in which reliable information may be compiled for the benefit of our audience.
